Payment Settings

Control how affiliates pay the once-off R100 joining fee.

Recommended while online payment is reviewing the affiliate subdomain: keep Manual Payment ON and online payment Payment OFF.
Shows a button on affiliate join/login pages for R100 manual payment instructions.
If ON, affiliate registration will show manual payment instructions instead of redirecting to online payment.
If ON, affiliate registration can continue to the online payment payment flow.
